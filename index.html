About Me

Hi, I'm Lewis! I'm currently an undergraduate student at the University of Manchester, in the UK, studying for a Computer Science degree. I've always had an interest in computers, ever since I first started playing with Scratch in primary school. Since then, I've created a few different projects, some of which I will post on this website. Any future projects I make may also eventually appear here, now that I have a clear understanding of how the development process works.
I have learned a lot about both the world of computing and about my self whilst at University. I have met a huge number of people, all with different motivations, life stories, and approaches to life. My approach to life is that you are made of everything you experience: you take parts of your surroundings, your experiences with the world, and you make that a part of you. Through this, I have come to discover new ways to deal with problems, ways to communicate with others, and how to work well in unfamiliar situations.


Interests

My primary areas of interest include:

  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Computer Vision
  • Machine Learning
  • Software Development

As well as computing projects, I also have an interest in art. My main art style is physical, creating paintings, drawings, and more recently crochet and amigurumi. These are all ways for me to express my creativity, and create work that I'm happy with. For me, art is used for fun:

It doesn't need to be "good". It doesn't need to be something that other people like. Art is for the creator, and should always be for the creator.
